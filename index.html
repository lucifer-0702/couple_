Our Love Story

We're met

I met my girlfriend, Tanvi, for the first time at the Gurudev Temple in her hometown of Saduthla. The moment was filled with excitement and nervousness, as I eagerly anticipated our meeting. Standing there, surrounded by the serene atmosphere of the temple, my heart raced with a mix of joy and apprehension. Seeing her for the first time made all my nerves fade away, and I felt an instant connection that made the moment truly special. It was a memorable experience that marked the beginning of our beautiful journey together.

First Date

Our first date was at Umiya Fastfood Center in Visnagar, a day etched in my heart forever. We indulged in delicious dabeli, bhel chat, and a very special ice cream, each bite sweeter with her by my side. The highlight of our date came when I mustered the courage to kiss her hand for the first time. As my lips gently touched her skin, my heart soared, blooming like a valley of flowers in spring. The warmth of that moment enveloped us, making our connection even stronger and more magical. It was a beautiful beginning to our romantic journey, filled with love and unforgettable memories.

Future planning

Our future together is a tapestry woven with dreams and promises, each thread representing our deep love and commitment. Tanvi and I envision a life of calm and healthy long-term relationship, cherishing every moment and growing stronger with each passing day. Our hearts are set on exploring the world together, embarking on countless adventures and creating memories in every corner of the globe. The most cherished dream of all is our plan to marry after seven beautiful years of togetherness, sealing our bond with a lifelong vow. Our future is a love story in the making, filled with passion, joy, and unwavering devotion.

Heer and Ranjha, our love story finds its heart in the serene Sector 28 Garden in Gandhinagar, our favorite place to visit. This garden, where we’ve met countless times, holds a special place in our hearts, symbolizing the many beautiful moments we’ve shared. The peaceful surroundings and lush greenery provide the perfect backdrop for our endless conversations and laughter. Just a short stroll away is the Cartoon Restaurant, where we indulge in our favorite dishes like dry paneer, shahi paneer, paneer toofani, dal fry with jeera rice, and crispy french fries. Each visit is a delightful blend of romance and culinary adventure, making every meal a celebration of our love. These cherished outings to the garden and restaurant have created a tapestry of memories, adding a touch of magic to our beautiful journey together.
